    DASHBOARD

    Website Modules

    PORTFOLIO
    The portfolio module is a collection of all your personal details. You can view the details of your profile.
    TRANSACTIONS
    This module provides you with a collection of all your investments. You can view the details of each investment and the total value of your investments.
    SECURITIES
    This module provides you with all the details concerning the securities (both opened and closed). You can view the details of each bond including their maturity dates, date of auction and their face values.
    BIDS
    This module enables you to place bids on the opened bonds. You are required to provide your details and the details of the bond itself in order to successfully place a bid.
    PAYMENTS
    This module enables users to fulfill their payments for the bonds successfully placed. You are required to provide your details of payment and the details of the bond itself in order to successfully pay for a bid placed.
